Welcome to Wheeler Coding Club!

We are a community of coders who help each other learn.

OUR MISSION

Coding Club is dedicated to creating a community within Wheeler High School that lets members learn about and discuss any topics involving coding or programming.

We abide by the 3 C's: Coding, Community, and Collaboration.

THE 3 C'S

Coding

Coding Club provides resources for coders trying to learn more! You can check out our resources here.

We encourage and facilitate project-based learning.

Community

We have a community of coders. This means that when you join, you can meet new people, ask for help on your projects, and discuss tech.

Collaboration

We foster collaboration within our community. You can share your ideas, collaborate on projects, and share your final projects.

JOIN US!


If you're interested in coding and currently attend Wheeler High School, you can join Wheeler Coding Club by filling out the form below.

GUEST SPEAKERS


If you have experience in a computer science field and want to speak to students about computer science, please reach out to us! We're always grateful for professionals willing to share their experiences in computer science fields.

Please contact us at wheelercodingclub@gmail.com
